The Magical Mango Tree

Hello, I am a mango tree. I was a shelter to many birds and animals. I produce many mangoes. Many monkeys come and eat my mangoes. They also drop many leaves for other animals to eat. I was happily living there. Oh! I forgot to tell that where I lived. I lived in a jungle. But once a woodcutter named Ashok was coming towards the jungle where I lived, Ashok was a very wicked man. He was coming towards me with two big axes both in his hands. He was just coming towards me to cut me down but suddenly all the animals gathered around me to protect me from cutting down. But he killed all the animals. When he started cutting me down his axe broke into pieces. Then he used his other axe to cut me down but the same thing happened with the other axe also it broke into pieces. What is happening to my axe? Why it is not cutting the tree? Ashok said angrily.
